## About the Project

Bezawaryjni is a self-service automotive workshop combining professional mechanical service with DIY workstation rental. The website was created to:
- Attract both customers seeking professional service and those wanting to rent workstations
- Streamline booking and consultation processes
- Build trust through transparent service information and pricing

## Challenges

- Communicating two distinct service offerings (professional service + self-service workshops)
- Integration with booking system (AutoNova)
- Mobile-first optimization for quick contact and booking
- Content management (services, pricing, workshop information)
- High CTR on booking and contact buttons

## Solution

### Technical Architecture

Built on **Next.js** with focus on:
- **Server-side rendering** – better SEO for local searches
- **Static generation** – fast loading for all users
- **Headless CMS** – easy content management for services and descriptions

### Page Sections

#### 1. Hero Section
Clearly communicates value proposition: self-service workshop + professional service. CTA links directly to booking system.

#### 2. Our Services
- Computer diagnostics
- Brake system repair
- Vehicle mechanics
- Oil and fluid changes
- Suspension service

Each service includes an icon and brief description for quick user navigation.

#### 3. News Section
Displays latest updates (e.g., "Two new hydraulic lifts installed"), positioning the workshop as dynamic and growing.

#### 4. Why Us?
Differentiating features:
- Honest, upfront pricing
- Fast turnaround and constant communication
- OEM and premium parts
- Option to work on your own vehicle

#### 5. Partners
Integrations with Inter Cars and AutoPartner, demonstrating professionalism and access to genuine parts.

#### 6. FAQ
Answering common questions:
- Can I work on my vehicle in the workshop?
- What parts do you use?
- Do you provide warranties?

#### 7. Contact Form
Main conversion channel with fields: Name, Email, Phone (optional), Message.

#### 8. Location Map
Embedded Google Map with workshop address.

### Conversion Optimization

- **Multiple CTAs** – booking buttons in hero, service sections, and footer
- **Contact numbers** – telephone links in header and footer
- **Responsive design** – excellent mobile experience (70% of traffic)
- **Speed optimization** – .avif images, lazy loading
- **Social proof** – customer reviews (4.8/5 rating)

## Results

- Page load time: ~1.5s (Lighthouse 95+)
- Booking conversion increase: +35% CTR improvement through multi-channel CTAs
- Mobile-first approach: 70% traffic from mobile devices
- SEO: Top rankings for local searches "automotive repair [city]"
